<objective>
Build AI structure detection and variable system that transforms raw text into editable persuasion blocks with auto-detected variables.

Purpose: The magic of Upload-First is that AI understands document structure semantically. A section about "47 e-commerce brands we've helped" becomes a "credibility" block. A sentence with company name becomes a variable placeholder. This plan implements that intelligence.

Output: Structure detection service, variable detection, interpolation service, variable picker UI.
</objective>
