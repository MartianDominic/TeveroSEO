- `setupFeeCents: integer("setup_fee_cents")`

- `unitLabel: text("unit_label")` (e.g., "per article")
- `inclusions: jsonb("inclusions").$type<string[]>()` (what's included)
- `termsTemplate: text("terms_template")` (legal clause)
- `termsTemplateEn: text("terms_template_en")`, `termsTemplateLt: text("terms_template_lt")`
